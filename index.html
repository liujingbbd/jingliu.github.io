Jing Liu (刘 静)

A mind who enjoys reading, thinking, imagining and trying

Contact: liujingbbd@gmail.com

Hi, welcome! I am Jing, a second year Master student of Institute of Network Technology, Beijing University of Posts and Communications, also a member of Next Generation Internet Laboratory in Tsinghua University, advised by Professor Yong Cui. I am broadly interested in networking, systems and security, with a strong emphasis on the intersection with machine learning, deep learning and reinforcement learning. My recent research focuses on TCP congestion control with deep reinforcement learning. Last and most importantly, my mission is to build models and algorithms to power networked systems.

Before joining BUPT, I got my Bachelor degree in Communication Engineering from Harbin University of Science and Technology in 2016. I was a member of Embedded System Lab, supervised by Prof. Dongming Li. I was obsessed with embedded system and hardware, mainly about operation system of wireless router and network interface card(NIC).

Research Experience

Research Assistant(Advisor: Prof. Yong Cui)
October 2015 - Present

Computer Science Department of Tsinghua University

TCP Congestion Control + Deep Reinforcement Learning

Designed and implemented an experience-driven multiobjective congestion control algorithm based on deep deterministic policy gradient collaborated with researchers of HUAWEI(this project is also funded by HUAWEI) & Tsinghua University, and measured many the state-of-the-art congestion control algorithms' performance under emulated scenarios. Paper

My Master degree thesis are based on this project.

Video Cache + Optimization Algorithm

Analyzed cache placement mechanism for the popular videos to achieve maximal utility function, abstracted this practical problem into a 0-1 multiple knapsack problem, researched many classic and mathematical optimization solutions and finally designed a greedy algorithm to solve the problem. Paper Code

Security + Machine Learning

Researched enormous cutting-edge papers of cyberspace security based on machine learning, summarized these papers’ research methodology and wrote a literature review collaborated with a Ph.D. of Tsinghua University. I was responsible for the section of system security and application security. Paper

Data Center Networking + Deep Learning

Read papers about deep learning and DCN topology, deployed a distributed computing environment using hadoop, trained a deep learning algorithm based on caffe, and measured performance for the deep learning-generated topology. Paper

Research Assistant(Advisor: Prof. Dongming Li)
May 2014 - June 2016

Embedded System Lab of Harbin University of Science and Technology

Video Fusion of Two Cameras Based on Embedded System

First, I compiled OperWrt_widora system, by adding opencv lib, I could develop application which can stitch images from two cameras and generate video. Finally, after adding ffmpeg and ffserver to the system, we can watch the fusion video of two cameras which pluged on the embedded board over web. Code

My Bachelor thesis was based on this project.

Internet radio based on wireless router

A funny widget on the router system--OpenWrt, which can be used to listen to Internet radio via an embedded development board and a sound card. I compiled the kernel, made the driver and fixed it to a fixed-point decoding algorithm. Code

IoT development board based on RTL8196C(a NIC)

It’s a commercial product incubated from laboratory for the education of embedded system, its product life was during 2015 - 2018 that you can buy it on Taobao. My advisor Prof. Li developed its hardware, and I developed its software system & applications based on RTL8196-SDK(kernel source code), including kernel, driver, tcp/udp communication with cloud and so on, and wrote a mannul to help more people learn embedded system better. Code
