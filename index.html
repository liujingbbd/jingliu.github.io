Hi, welcome! I am Jing, a second year Master student of Institute of Network Technology, Beijing University of Posts and Communications, also a member of Next Generation Internet Laboratory in Tsinghua University, advised by Professor Yong Cui. I am broadly interested in networking, systems and security, with a strong emphasis on the intersection with machine learning, deep learning and reinforcement learning. My recent research focuses on TCP congestion control with deep reinforcement learning. Last and most importantly, my mission is to build models and algorithms to power networked systems.

Before joining BUPT, I got my Bachelor degree in Communication Engineering from Harbin University of Science and Technology in 2016. I was a member of Embedded System Lab, supervised by Prof. Dongming Li. I was obsessed with embedded system and hardware, mainly about operation system of wireless router and network interface card(NIC).
